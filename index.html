Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download my eBook?

After purchase, you'll receive a download link via email. For Apple Books, the book will appear in your library automatically. For Kindle, it will be delivered to your registered device.

My book isn't downloading. What should I do?

First, check your internet connection and try again. If the issue persists, ensure you're signed into the correct account. For further assistance, contact me at the email below.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by the platform where you purchased the book (Apple Books, Amazon Kindle, etc.). Please contact their support directly for refund requests.

Will there be print editions available?

Print editions are planned for select titles. Sign up for the newsletter below to be notified when they become available.
